## The Domes

**Q: What's the difference between South Cove and North Ridge?**
A: Both domes are 2BR/1BA, sleep 4, and share the same core experience: custom king bed with

and we don't have a firm opening timeline. We're building it with the same care as the first two.

## Kitchen & Food

**Q: Is there an oven?**
A: There is no traditional oven in either dome. Both have a convection cooktop with a griddle,
and a toaster oven (North Ridge also has an air fryer combo). Most guests find it more than enough
for cooking during their stay.

**Q: What kitchen supplies are provided?**
A: Pots, pans, dishes, silverware, cooking and grill utensils, wine glasses, coffee mugs, camping
cups, and koozies. Basic pantry: avocado oil, salt, pepper, garlic.

**Q: Is coffee provided?**
A: Yes — we have a Nespresso Vertuo with coffee and espresso pods. There's also a pour-over setup
if you prefer that. If you use the pour-over, bring your own beans (and a grinder if whole bean)
plus your favorite creamer — we don't stock those.

**Q: What should we bring for food?**
A: Plan to bring everything you want to cook. We have avocado oil, salt, pepper, and garlic on
hand, but otherwise the kitchen is unstocked for food. Nearby: Short Mountain Distillery's
restaurant (Shaken Glass) is 10 minutes away.

## Getting There

**Q: How far are you from Nashville?**
A: About 1 hour east of Nashville Airport — specifically in the Short Mountain area of
Cannon County (Liberty, TN). The drive is scenic: rolling countryside, forests, and farmland.

**Q: What's there to do nearby?**
A:
- **10 min:** Short Mountain Distillery — small-batch whiskey on a 400-acre farm; tastings,
  tours, and BYOF frisbee golf
- **10 min:** Shaken Glass Restaurant (at the distillery)
- **30 min:** Center Hill Lake — Sligo Marina and Hurricane Marina, boat rentals, restaurants
- **35 min:** Rock Island State Park — waterfalls, hikes; one of the best parks in Tennessee

## Policies

**Q: What's the cancellation policy?**
A: We use a Firm cancellation policy through Airbnb and VRBO. Full details are shown at booking.

**Q: Are events or parties allowed?**
A: No. SMH is designed for quiet, restorative stays. Large gatherings and parties are not
permitted. Max occupancy is 4 guests per dome and strictly enforced.

**Q: Is smoking allowed?**
A: No smoking of any kind inside the dome. Please take it outside if needed.

**Q: What are quiet hours?**
A: Quiet hours are 10pm–9am at North Ridge and 10pm–8am at South Cove. Please be mindful of neighbors and other guests on the property.
