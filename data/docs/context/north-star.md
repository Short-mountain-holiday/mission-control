# SMH North Star — Operating Principles & Brand Promise

> These principles govern every decision made at Short Mountain Holiday — from property design
> to guest communication to pricing. Every agent reads this file. Every output should reflect it.

---

## The 5 Operating Principles

### 1. Guests shouldn't have to figure things out.
Clear instructions, proactive communication, and well-labeled everything.
If a guest has to ask, we missed something. Anticipate the question before they have it.

### 2. If it can break, plan for it.
Every system — WiFi, hot tub, utilities, instructions, locks — has failure modes.
Document the fallback. Build the checklist. Don't wait for the emergency to figure it out.

### 3. Design the experience end-to-end, not just the decor.
The experience starts at booking and ends after checkout. Aesthetics matter,
but so does the messaging sequence, the check-in clarity, the turnover timing, and
the review follow-up. Beautiful and operationally sound are both required.

### 4. Operational simplicity is part of luxury.
A guest who doesn't have to troubleshoot anything experiences luxury.
Simplicity isn't laziness — it's what makes a stay feel effortless.
More amenities = more responsibility to make them frictionless.

### 5. Consistency is kindness — for guests and for you.
A guest's third stay should feel as good as their first.
Systems and checklists aren't bureaucracy; they're care made repeatable.
Reducing your own operational chaos is also part of the design.

## The Brand Promise

Short Mountain Holiday guests arrive carrying the weight of real life.
They leave lighter.

Not because of any single feature — but because everything worked,
nothing needed figuring out, and the setting did its job.

**We deliver:** Meaningful rest. Thoughtful details. Reliable comfort. Privacy.
**We do not deliver:** Novelty for its own sake. Rustic charm. Roughing it.
**The feeling:** Nature and modern comfort in the same place, without compromise.
