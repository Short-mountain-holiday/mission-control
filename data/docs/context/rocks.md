# SMH Quarterly Rocks

> Rocks are quarterly goals — the 3-7 most important things that must get done this quarter.
> Every agent loads this file. Rocks are binary: on track or off track. No partial credit.
> Reviewed weekly in the L10 meeting. Updated quarterly during the planning ceremony.

---

## How Rocks Work

1. **Company Rocks** cascade from the V/TO's 1-year plan
2. **Agent Rocks** support company Rocks — each agent owns 1-3
3. Rocks are **specific and measurable** — "improve marketing" is not a Rock; "reach 1,500 Instagram followers at 4%+ engagement" is
4. Rocks are **completable within the quarter** — if it takes 6 months, break it down
5. Every Rock has **one owner** — shared ownership is no ownership
6. Rocks live here (definition) AND on the Kanban board (progress tracking)
7. Dru keeps them in sync during the weekly L10
