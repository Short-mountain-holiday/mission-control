## South Cove Dome

### Identity
The original dome. Named for its position on the property. Smart TV hidden in a motorized cabinet
that emerges at the push of a button. Loft has two twin memory foam beds that can be pushed
together for an additional king. Known for intimate, tucked-in feel.

### Key Features
- Custom king bed with panoramic views (doubles as couch during day)
- Bistro table — rolls over for "breakfast in bed"
- Smart TV in a moveable cabinet (emerges with a button press)
- Private hot tub with views
- Gas fire pit with Adirondack rocking chairs
- Gas grill with large prep area
- Deck designed for privacy + views
- Light-filled bathroom: rainwater showerhead, bathrobes, standard flush toilet
- Hook-and-ring game on deck, cornhole in driveway

### Kitchen
- Convection cooktop with griddle
- Toaster oven (**no traditional oven**)
- Fridge
- Nespresso Vertuo with coffee and espresso pods
- Pour-over setup (BYOB — bring your own beans)
- Stocked: pots, pans, dishes, silverware, cooking + grill utensils, camping cups, koozies, wine glasses, coffee mugs
- Basic pantry: avocado oil, salt, pepper, garlic

### Connectivity
- Fiber WiFi
- Full cell service (data + voice, all major carriers)
- WiFi network: **Short Mountain Holiday** (same for both domes)

### What to Bring (Recommended)
- Extra layers (fall/winter)
- Hiking shoes + slip-ons
- Bag of ice (ice trays in freezer but limited)
- Favorite creamer + whole beans if using pour-over
- Food for meals (pantry basics provided)
